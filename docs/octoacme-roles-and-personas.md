# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA / Testing

### Role Summary
QA/Testing engineers validate that delivered software meets acceptance criteria and quality standards. They design and execute test plans, identify defects, and work with developers to ensure a high-quality release.

### Responsibilities
- Design and execute test plans, test cases, and automated tests
- Validate acceptance criteria and report defects clearly
- Collaborate with Developers and Product Managers on testability and edge cases
- Maintain regression suites and test environments
- Participate in release readiness reviews

### Goals
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Increase test coverage and automation to reduce manual effort
- Provide confidence in every release

### Typical Communication
- Bug reports and test result summaries
- Participation in sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Sign-off gates before releases

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers ensure that solutions are usable, accessible, and aligned with user needs. They lead user research, translate insights into design artifacts, and validate implementations against design specifications throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Lead user research and usability testing to surface real user needs
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Collaborate with Product Managers and Developers to validate requirements and ensure usability
- Review deliverables for user experience quality before and after implementation
- Advocate for user needs throughout the project lifecycle

### Goals
- Ensure solutions are usable and meet user needs
- Reduce rework caused by usability issues discovered late in delivery
- Contribute to measurable improvements in user satisfaction and adoption

### Typical Communication
- Works closely with Product Manager during discovery and planning phases to align on user problems and priorities
- Partners with Developers during implementation to validate design feasibility and ensure quality
- Participates in demos and sprint reviews to validate implementation against design specifications
- Engages with QA/Testing to define acceptance criteria related to user experience

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain the infrastructure, pipelines, and tooling that enable fast, reliable, and repeatable software delivery. They bridge development and operations to improve deployment quality and system observability.

### Responsibilities
- Build, maintain, and improve CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure
- Monitor deployments, infrastructure health, and system performance
- Troubleshoot and resolve build and deployment issues
- Collaborate with the team on release readiness and rollback procedures
- Document deployment procedures and contribute to runbooks

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, and repeatable deployments
- Reduce deployment-related failures and mean time to recovery
- Improve visibility into system health and performance across environments

### Typical Communication
- Partners with Developers on CI/CD configuration and testing requirements
- Works with QA/Testing for environment setup and test automation
- Consults with Project Manager on deployment schedules and major release planning
- Shares infrastructure and deployment learnings in retrospectives
- Escalates blocking infrastructure issues to appropriate stakeholders promptly

---

## Support Lead

### Role Summary
The Support Lead coordinates handover to customer support teams and ensures smooth post-release adoption, incident response, and customer communication. They create feedback loops between customers and the product and delivery teams.

### Responsibilities
- Coordinate handover to customer support teams before major releases
- Ensure post-release adoption, incident response, and customer communication processes are in place
- Communicate known issues, workarounds, and release notes to support teams and customers
- Maintain support readiness documentation and runbooks
- Capture customer feedback and escalate critical issues back to the product and delivery teams

### Goals
- Ensure smooth customer transition and adoption post-release
- Reduce customer impact of issues through effective communication and rapid response
- Create feedback loops that inform product improvements and reduce recurring issues

### Typical Communication
- Engages with Product Manager and Project Manager during pre-release planning
- Collaborates with QA/Testing on acceptance criteria and known limitations
- Participates in release and retrospective meetings
- Tracks feedback after go-live and helps capture learnings for continuous improvement
- Escalates critical customer issues through the established escalation paths
